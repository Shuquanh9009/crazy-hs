Modern Generation of H.S Students

In the 21st century there are many types of students. There are students that are academically driven. While other students are caught up in their feelings or relationships. These students have a huge impact on their academic lives.

  • Gamer/Antisocial students: These students are focused in the world through a computer screen,motherboard, and processor.
  • Academically driven scholars: Friends? The only friends they spend more time with is a textbook and an graphic calculator.
  • Athletic Students: You would notice these big giants in the hallways and in the classroom talking about practice and personal stats.
  • Drama/Relationship students: They spill tea and don't clean up afterwards.
